SERVO BEHAVIOR:
===============
HIP servos (0,1,4,5):
  - 90° = straight sideways
  - Lower angle = forward
  - Higher angle = backward

LEG servos (2,3,6,7):
  - 90° = straight down
  - 45° = lift one way
  - 135° = lift other way
